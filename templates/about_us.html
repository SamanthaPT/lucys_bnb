So, who's Lucy?

My husband Joe and I, as Portuguese immigrants in North America, moved back to our beautiful island to raise our children. Now that they‘re all grown up and have "abandoned" us, we have transformed our home into a place where we can receive people from all over the world from so many different cultures.

We are friendly islander folk who cater to our guests' every need and make them feel welcome. We invite you to stay with us during your trip to Pico island.

Besides... we have pancakes!
